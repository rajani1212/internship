Documenting Requirements

Once the requirements are analyzed and understood, they need to be documented properly. This phase involves writing down all the requirements in detail, including what the product should do (functional requirements), how it should perform (non-functional requirements), and any technical specifications that need to be considered.

Documnet Requirement steps

  1. Define project scope and objectives.
  2. Gather business and stakeholder requirements.
  3. Document requirements.
  4. Review and validate requirements.
  5. Monitor and control requirements.
