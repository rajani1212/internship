Managing Requirements

Requirement management is an ongoing process throughout the product development lifecycle. It involves keeping track of changes to requirements, ensuring that they are up to date, and managing any conflicts or scope changes that may arise. This phase focuses on maintaining the quality and integrity of the requirements from start to finish.

Benefits of requirements management

  • Lower cost of development across the lifecycle
  • Fewer defects
  • Minimized risk for safety-critical products
  • Faster delivery
  • Reusability
  • Traceability
  • Requirements tied to test cases
  • Global configuration management
