Design Thinking

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that emphasizes understanding user needs, defining problems, ideating solutions, prototyping, and testing. It involves empathy for users, creative problem-solving, and iterative design processes.

Design thinking encourages multidisciplinary collaboration, iteration, and a focus on user experience to develop products that truly meet user needs. By incorporating user feedback throughout the design process, design thinking helps ensure that the final product is user-centric and impactful
