Corrective Maintenance

Under corrective maintenance, maintenance teams get to work as soon as a problem occurs. The goal of corrective maintenance is to bring systems back to regular operation as quickly as possible. With corrective maintenance, there is no program for regular maintenance. A problem must be present before maintenance occurs.

Example

Repairing a broken HVAC unit rather than maintaining it.
Repairing an HVAC unit after data from the unit shows it is not functioning at peak performance.
