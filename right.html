PRODUCT ENGINEERING

Product engineering is the process of turning an idea into a fully manufacturable product.Product engineering is a process of applying engineering principles, design thinking, and project management to improve user experience, software quality and enable businesses to deploy high-performance systems.

Phases of Product Engineering

  1. Planning or Ideation
  2. Requirement Analysis
  3. Product Design
  4. Product Development
  5. Product Testing or Quality Assurance
  6. Product Deployment
  7. Product Maintenance
